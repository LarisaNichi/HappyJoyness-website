Play with Boby

I am Boby, the sweetest dog you've ever seen.

I never refuse a good meal and I am addicted to cheese.

I search for cold places during the hot summer days.

Water is my biggest enemy.

After I take a bath, I get revenged and I cover myself with mud.

I like being scratched and I am always asking for more.

Barbeque

Make a barbeque in a relaxing place where time stopped.

Listen to the sound of wood crackling in the fire.

Enjoy the sunset and dream about tomorrow.

Cover yourself with the heat of the embers in a deep silence.

Feel the joy and the happiness inside you.

Sightseeing

Discover the beauties of nature and find the secret of life itself.

Let yourself be embraced by the sound of infinite and tickled by the wind.

Walk deep into the woods where there are doors to new worlds.

Let the stress disappear and find the way to your soul.

Eat healthy

Discover the flavors of the forest fruits picked by you from the wildest places.

Remember forever the rich and mysterious aroma experience given by the wild strawberries, raspberries and blackberries.

Try the unique taste of the hazelnuts in the wild, with their nutty, creamy, earthy and smooth buttery flavor.

Meet animals

Build strengths

Take care of the roses from the garden and help the vegetables grow.

Enjoy the wild experience of raking the hay.

Become one with the nature that gave us strengths to take care of its goods at any age.

Return to the beginning of time and discover the way that life started years ago.
